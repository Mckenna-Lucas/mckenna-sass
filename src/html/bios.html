Biography of Harvey Dale and the Cements

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale started singing at age 3 and was noticed by BigMachine records as a singing compeition when she was 12. She started the Cements two years later.

Winnie Ryan

Winnie Ryan was childhood friends with Harvey Dale and they grew up listening to Stevie Nicks and Tina Turner. She started playing the drums in middle school band. She's come along way from playing in the stands of football games.
